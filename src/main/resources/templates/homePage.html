Rules for working with the service

1. To use the service, you must sign in via Google. To do this, there is a button in the right corner of the navigation bar.

2. When you click on a brand, you will be returned to this hint.

3. The "Create meeting" tab is used to add your own meetings.

4. The "All my meetings" tab is used to view all meetings.

5. The "Current meetings " tab is used for editing current meetings.

6. To sign out, click the button in the right corner again.
